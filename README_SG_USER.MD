This guide explains how a user can search for ingredients and get recipe results using the Recipe Finder application.

---

## 1. Open the Recipe Finder
After running the application on your computer, a webpage will open in your browser.

You will see a simple page with a **search bar** in the center.

---

## 2. Enter Your Ingredients
Click on the search bar and type the ingredients you have at home.

Example inputs:
eggs
milk, sugar
chicken, rice, tomato

yaml
Copy code

You can enter:
- one ingredient  
- multiple ingredients  
- ingredients separated by commas  

---

## 3. Start the Search
After typing your ingredients, click the **Search** or **Find Recipes** button.

---

## 4. View Your Results
The system will automatically check your ingredients and display recipes that match what you entered.

The results page will show:
- recipe names  
- ingredients included  
- simple steps or instructions  

If no recipes match, the page will show a message telling you that nothing was found.

---

## 5. Try Again Anytime
To search for new recipes:
1. Go back to the search bar  
2. Enter different ingredients  
3. Click the button again  

The page will update with new results each time you search.

---

## 6. Summary
- Type your ingredients into the search bar  
- Click **Find Recipes**  
- View all matching recipe suggestions  
- Search again anytime by entering new items
